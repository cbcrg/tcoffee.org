Notredame's Lab

The main focus of our laboratory is the development of novel algorithms for the comparison of multiple biological sequences. The goal is allowing comparisons based on any relevant biological signal such as sequence homology, structural similarity, genomic structure, functional similarity and more generally any signal that may be identified within biological sequences.
