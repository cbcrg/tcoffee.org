USING SAGA
    SAGA requires a powerful UNIX station to run. It is optimally designed for aligning less than 20 sequences, less than 400 residues long. At present we have no plan to port it on any non-UNIX platform. In a close future, I will support a small FAQ on SAGA including alternative parameter setting for solving different types of problems. Stay in touch and let me know about your needs. E-mail me any trouble you may encounter including SAGA: BUG REPORT in your mail tiltle. I will do my best to fix it :-).

    SAGA now also includes the new COFFEE objective function that is based on pairwise alignments consistency. As well as the GA based optimisation, SAGA allows users to do a greedy Tree based optimisation (T-COFFEE) that is much much much faster but less accurate....
